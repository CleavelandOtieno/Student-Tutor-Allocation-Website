Objectives

We wish to Provide qualified, trained tutors for one-on-one and group consultation,provide tutoring which supports the academic standards of the university,support learning in a positive and caring atmosphere,promote independent learning,foster confidence and self-esteem while Offering self-paced, computerized, and individualized instruction in a technology rich environment and refer students to appropriate support resources.

We want to help students understand modes of communication:Face toFace,word processing,phone conversing and messaging,instant messaging,synchronous and asynchronous collaboration with video and audio content.We also help in navigating research/academic Resources.

Goal

1.Encourage Engagement and Collaboration with Academic Community by understanding appropriate use of social media and collaborative word processing technologies,learning how to negotiate relationships including tutoring, advising, and faculty and understanding procrastination and motivational theory (e.g. failure avoiders).

2. We wish to enable our students cultivate life-long learning in an atmosphere that is positive and caring, supports classroom instruction and overall student success, and challenge and motivates individuals as they strive for and attain personal and academic growth.
